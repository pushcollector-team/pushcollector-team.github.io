Privacy Policy

PushCollector — a research project by CX Family
Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Overview

PushCollector is an Android application developed as part of the CX Family research project. The app is designed to study the perception of advertising push notifications.

This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use, store, and protect it.

By using the application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Push notification data: title, text, and image of push notifications only from apps you have selected for monitoring. We do not intercept notifications from apps you have not selected.
  • Google account data: your email address and Google account display name — for participant identification in the research.
  • Nickname: a pseudonym you enter during registration — used instead of your real name in research tables.
  • Notification ratings: tags you assign to push notifications as part of the research evaluation.
We do not collect location data, contacts, SMS, photos, files, call history, or any other personal data not listed above.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Research on the perception of advertising push notifications
  • Building a shared pool of notifications for evaluation by participants
  • Analytics on advertising push effectiveness
  • Participant identification within the research project

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Where Data Is Transferred

Data is transferred to the following services:

  • Google Sheets: push notifications (in "research" mode), nickname, ratings, and email are stored in a private spreadsheet of the research project. Access to the spreadsheet is restricted to the research organizers.
  • Cloudinary: images from push notifications are uploaded to Cloudinary cloud storage for display in the shared evaluation pool.

Data in "personal" mode is not transferred to external services and remains on your device.

5. Data Storage

  • On device: data is stored in a local Room database (SQLite). Sensitive data (email, tokens) is stored using EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • In the cloud: data is stored in Google Sheets (private spreadsheet) and Cloudinary (images).

We do not set a fixed data retention period. Data is retained for the duration of the research project.

6. Data Protection

  • All network requests are made over HTTPS (cleartext traffic is disabled).
  • Authentication is performed via OAuth 2.0 (Google Sign-In).
  • The app requests only the minimum necessary permissions (principle of least privilege).
  • Debug logs are stripped in release builds via ProGuard.

7. Data Deletion

  • Local data: you can clear app data through Android settings or through the "Settings" section in the app.
  • Data in Google Sheets and Cloudinary: to fully delete your data from cloud storage, email artemmerzliakov@gmail.com with your nickname and email address.

We will process deletion requests within 14 business days.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of the research at any time (disable data collection)
  • Choose which apps to monitor
  • Use "personal" mode (data is not synced to the cloud)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request information about what data we store about you

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In case of significant changes, we will notify participants through the application.

The current version of this policy is always available at this address.
